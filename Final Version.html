Basic Info

Wil grew up in Hampden, Maine and goes to the University of Maine in Orono. He works at Brick Oven Bangor Rye Bakery and delivers pizza for Orono House of Pizza. He has a loving family and a sister who lives in Portland Oregon. In his free time he likes to play sports and hangout with his friends.

Wil has had many life experiences including traveling to Mexico, Canada, and the mid west. He has seen the Rockie Mountains in Montana and skiied in Vale, Colorado. He has climbed Mount Katadhin 10 times and planes to travel to Europe in the next 3 years.
